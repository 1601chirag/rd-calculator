RD Calculator

This RD calculator helps you calculate the maturity amount of a recurring deposit easily. It is useful for understanding how monthly savings grow over time with interest.

Recurring Deposit (RD) is an investment where you deposit a fixed amount every month for a fixed period and earn interest on it.

Calculate Recurring Deposit Maturity Amount










How RD Maturity Amount is Calculated

The maturity amount of a recurring deposit is calculated using a simplified interest formula based on monthly deposits and interest rate.

RD Maturity Amount = (Monthly Deposit × Number of Months)
+ Interest Earned on Monthly Deposits

This calculator uses a standard RD calculation method to give an approximate maturity value.

Example of RD Calculation

If you deposit ₹2,000 per month for 24 months at an interest rate of 6% per year, the maturity amount will be approximately ₹51,500.
